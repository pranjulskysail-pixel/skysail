Canada Citizenship By Investment

Canada does not offer a direct Citizenship by Investment program. However, entrepreneurs, investors, and business owners can obtain Permanent Residency through eligible business immigration pathways and later become eligible for Canadian Citizenship after meeting residency and legal requirements.

Why Pursue Canadian Citizenship?

Canadian Passport

Enjoy one of the world's strongest passports with visa-free travel to many countries.

Voting Rights

Participate in Canadian elections and democratic processes.

No Residency Renewal

Citizenship is permanent and does not require PR card renewals.

Family Security

Provide long-term security and opportunities for your family.

World-Class Benefits

Access healthcare, education, and social benefits.

Global Business Opportunities

Expand your personal and business opportunities internationally.

Benefits Of Canadian Citizenship

  • Canadian passport and global mobility
  • Voting and political participation rights
  • No PR card renewal requirements
  • Access to government benefits and protections
  • Ability to pass citizenship to eligible children
  • Long-term security and stability

Frequently Asked Questions

Does Canada offer direct Citizenship by Investment?

No. Canada currently does not have a direct citizenship-by-investment program.

Can investors become Canadian citizens?

Yes. Eligible investors may first obtain Permanent Residency and later apply for citizenship.

How long does it take to become a Canadian citizen?

The timeline depends on obtaining PR and meeting citizenship eligibility requirements.

Can my family be included?

Most business immigration programs allow eligible family members to be included.
